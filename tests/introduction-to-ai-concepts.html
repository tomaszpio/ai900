AI-900 Practice Test: Introduction to AI Concepts

1️⃣ What best describes Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

  • A. A system that stores large amounts of data.
  • B. A system that can simulate human reasoning and behavior.
  • C. A system that runs simple predefined rules.
  • D. A database for structured data storage.
Show Answer

✅ Correct Answer: B — A system that can simulate human reasoning and behavior.

Explanation: Artificial Intelligence (AI) enables machines to perform cognitive functions like learning, reasoning, and problem solving — similar to human intelligence.

2️⃣ Which of the following is an example of computer vision?

  • A. Translating text from English to French.
  • B. Detecting faces in a photograph.
  • C. Predicting customer churn.
  • D. Recognizing spoken commands.
Show Answer

✅ Correct Answer: B — Detecting faces in a photograph.

Explanation: Computer vision allows computers to interpret visual data such as images and videos to identify objects, people, or scenes.

3️⃣ Which Microsoft Responsible AI principle ensures systems do not discriminate against people?

  • A. Transparency
  • B. Fairness
  • C. Inclusiveness
  • D. Accountability
Show Answer

✅ Correct Answer: B — Fairness.

Explanation: The fairness principle ensures that AI systems treat all people equally and avoid bias in their predictions or outputs.

4️⃣ What is knowledge mining?

  • A. Training a model to recognize objects in images.
  • B. Extracting useful information from large sets of unstructured data.
  • C. Automating conversational responses.
  • D. Making predictions using historical data.
Show Answer

✅ Correct Answer: B — Extracting useful information from large sets of unstructured data.

Explanation: Knowledge mining uses AI and search technologies to extract insights from unstructured documents like PDFs or emails.

5️⃣ Which type of AI workload would be used to predict the future sales of a product?

  • A. Computer Vision
  • B. Natural Language Processing
  • C. Predictive Analytics / Forecasting
  • D. Anomaly Detection
Show Answer

✅ Correct Answer: C — Predictive Analytics / Forecasting.

Explanation: Predictive workloads use historical data and statistical models to forecast future outcomes like sales or demand.

6️⃣ What distinguishes narrow AI from general AI?

  • A. Narrow AI performs specific tasks; General AI can handle any intellectual task a human can.
  • B. Narrow AI is faster but less accurate.
  • C. General AI is based on simple rule-based systems.
  • D. There is no difference between them.
Show Answer

✅ Correct Answer: A — Narrow AI performs specific tasks; General AI can handle any intellectual task a human can.

Explanation: All current AI is “narrow” — specialized for one task, such as translation or image recognition. “General AI” (human-level) does not yet exist.
